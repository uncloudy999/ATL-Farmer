  5 Ways to Improve Soil Fertility

Ways to improve soil fertility are -
  1. Till the soil to loosen it for better aeration
  2. Add organic matter to your soil like vermicompost and farmyard manure.
  3. Grow leguminious crops like peas and beans for naturally adding nitrogen in the soil as they are nitrogen fixation crops.
  4. Mulch your soil to retain water, control weeds and prevent erosion.
  5. A range of microorganisms that support plant growth should be added
